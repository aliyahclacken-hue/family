How To Play

Turn VoiceOver off for touch-explore mode. Drag your finger around the room. Footsteps play while you move. When you reach an object, the voice says its name.

Use Interact after finding an object. Use Put Down while carrying something.

Babies under 12 months do not wander off. They stay where you placed them.

Continuous sounds keep playing: crying repeats until the need is solved; shower, vacuum, shop and park sounds can loop.
